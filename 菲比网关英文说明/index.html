    Smart Home Link

    Product Information

    • Website: www.qiachip.com
    • App Store Link: Smart Home Link

    Alarm Mechanism

    There are two states of alarm mechanism for arming and disarming: - In the arming state, the device is turned on to put the gateway into alarm state.

    Operating Temperature

    The operating temperature range is from 25℃ to +50℃.

    Adding Devices to iOS Devices

    1. Follow the operating instructions of iOS devices to add devices with network (iOS/iPadOS HomePod Apple TV).

    2. For example, HomePod:

      • Open the iOS phone "Home" APP.
      • Tap the "+" in the upper right corner.
      • Select "Add or scan accessories".
      • Place the phone close to HomePod to scan the device.
      • Add the device and follow the APP instructions to add the network.

    3. Master Gateway Bridge:

    4. Note: The gateway and HomePod must be on the same LAN, and the network should be enabled with IPv6.
    5. Steps:
      • Open the iOS phone "Home" APP.
      • Tap the "+" in the top right corner.
      • Select "Add or scan accessories".
      • Scan the Master QR code.
      • According to the prompts, assign the gateway in the network (The Zigbee device added in the first step).
      • Enter each room and assign devices to control.

    Note

    • After unbinding the Master gateway, you need to restore the factory settings before bridging iOS devices again.
